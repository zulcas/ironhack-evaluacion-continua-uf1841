Overcooked! All You Can Eat – Available Now On PS5 & Xbox Series X

Go back

Prepare your appetite – Overcooked! All You Can Eat is out now on PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X and Xbox Series S!

 

Overcooked, Overcooked 2 and all the DLC have been combined along with exclusive new content, in this beautiful remastered 4K definitive edition!

  • Play over 200 levels including seven completely new ones!
  • Play as 60 different chefs with 128 skins available between them – including three new chefs exclusive to All You Can Eat!
  • 60fps gameplay in stunning 4K resolution
  • Faster loading times
  • Accessibility features and all-new Assist Mode
  • New achievements!
PLAY ONLINE:

Weve rebuilt Overcooked! in the Overcooked! 2 engine, which means youll be able to team up with your friends online to take on the kitchens from the original game!

ACCESSIBILITY FEATURES & ASSIST MODE:

Overcooked! All You Can Eat also include new accessibility options and Assist Mode, helping any budding master chef experience the heat of the kitchen.

The assist mode includes these adjustments:

  • The ability to slow the timeouts on recipes
  • Increase the duration of rounds
  • The option to skip levels
  • Toggle order expiration off in the settings

The accessibility functions include:

  • A scalable user interface
  • Dyslexic-friendly text
  • Colour blindness options

NEW CHEFS & ALTERNATE SKINS:

Choose from 60 different chefs in our revamped chef select screen!

Theres also a total of 128 alternate skins available across the roster of chefs, like this fancy rainbow Narwhal and shiny gold Robot!

EXCLUSIVE NEW CONTENT:

The Peckish Rises is a brand new culinary adventure, exclusively available to play in Overcooked! All You Can Eat.

This freshly baked adventure includes seven brand new spooky kitchens and adds three chefs to the roster: The adorable Axolotl Chef, the less-than-fresh Unbread Chef, and the original omnivorous Overlord, Everpeckish Chef!

New kitchens also means new ways to cook, so chefs will be dodging fire, using cannons, and chopping with guillotines in an attempt to complete their orders on time and all while managing mouthfuls of iconic Overcooked! mayhem.

Overcooked! All You Can Eat is available to download digitally on Xbox Series X and Xbox Series S right now for 39.99 / 49.99 / $49.99.

Keep up to date with news, meet other chefs and enter exclusive giveaways in the official Overcooked Discord!
